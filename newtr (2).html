Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is a temple complex in Cambodia and the largest religious monument in the world.

Angkor Thom

The south gate of Angkor Thom is most popular with visitors, as it has been fully restored and many of the heads (mostly copies) remain in place. The gate is on the main road into Angkor Thom from Angkor Wat.

Banteay Srei

Banteay Srei or Banteay Srey (Khmer: បន្ទាយស្រី) is a 10th-century Cambodian temple dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva. Located in the area of Angkor, it lies near the hill of Phnom Dei, 25 km (16 mi) north-east.
